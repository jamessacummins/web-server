DaHuo

DaHuo was my first go at creating a working app for myself. Throughout my journey learning Chinese, I had realised that I would quite often see words that might be worth learning, but would be dismayed to find they were very rare obscure language. DaHuo lets you look up Chinese characters by the frequency of how often they occur in the language.
